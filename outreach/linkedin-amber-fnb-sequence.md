## LinkedIn DM Micro-Sequence – Amber Grant (Food & Beverage)

Use this with owners/founders of women-owned food businesses (cafes, bakeries, etc.) in the US.

---

### 1. Connection Request

> Hi {{first_name}},  
> I follow women-owned food businesses in {{state}}, and your {{business_type}} – {{business_name}} – caught my eye.  
> Would love to connect here.

Short, no pitch.

---

### 2. Message 1 – After Connection Accepted (Soft Intro)

Send 1–2 days after they accept.

> Hi {{first_name}}, thanks for connecting.  
>  
> Quick question – have you ever heard of the **Amber Grant**? It’s a **$10,000 monthly grant** for women-owned businesses, and this month they have a **Food & Beverage** focus (cafes, bakeries, food trucks, etc.).  
>  
> I run *Summit Grant Solutions* and help women owners like you turn their story and goals into a strong, submission-ready Amber Grant application draft. You still submit it yourself on their website – I just handle the writing.  
>  
> If you’re even a little curious, I’m happy to share how the **$99 draft** works.

---

### 3. Message 2 – If They Show Interest (“Yes”, “Tell me more”)

> Love it – thanks for the reply, {{first_name}}.  
>  
> Here’s how the **$99 Amber Grant draft** works:  
>  
> 1. You answer a short intake form (your story, goals, how you’d use the $10k).  
> 2. I use AI + human editing to turn that into a **submission-ready Amber Grant application** in 24–48 hours.  
> 3. You copy/paste the answers into the official Amber Grant portal and pay their small fee.  
>  
> No equity, no loans, and no long calls – you stay focused on {{business_name}}, I handle the writing.  
>  
> If you’d like to move forward, I’ll send two links:  
> \- A secure **$99 payment link**  
> \- The **intake form** (takes about 10–15 minutes)  
>  
> Want me to send those over?

---

### 4. Message 3 – If They Go Quiet After Interest

Send 3–5 days after Message 2 if no response.

> Hi {{first_name}},  
>  
> Just checking in in case my last message got buried.  
>  
> The current **Amber Grant Food & Beverage** round closes at the end of the month, so there’s still time to get a strong draft done.  
>  
> If now’s not a good time, no worries at all – I can always loop you in on future grant rounds that fit {{business_name}}.  
>  
> Would you like me to keep you posted on **women-focused private grants** that match what you’re building?

---

### 5. Message 4 – Light Nurture (If They’re Not Ready Yet)

> Totally understand, {{first_name}} – timing is everything.  
>  
> I’ll keep an eye out for **women-owned food/retail grants** that look like a good fit for {{business_name}} and can ping you when something relevant opens.  
>  
> In the meantime, if you ever want a **quick, no-pressure look** at your story from a grant perspective, I’m happy to give a few pointers.  
>  
> Cheering you on from here.
