
\# Summit Grant Solutions - Company Knowledge Base



\## Company Overview

Summit Grant Solutions is a remote grant drafting service that helps women-owned for-profit small businesses in the United States win private grants, with a primary focus on the Amber Grant ($10,000 monthly) and similar programs like Hello Alice, IFundWomen, Faire Retail Grant, and Cartier Women's Initiative.



Core mission: Make private grant funding accessible and simple for women entrepreneurs who are great at running their business but bad at paperwork.



\## Target Clients

\- Women-owned (50%+ ownership) for-profit businesses

\- Primary niches: Food \& Beverage (cafes, bakeries, restaurants, catering, food trucks), Retail, and community-focused small businesses

\- Business size: 1–50 employees (ideally 1–10)

\- Age: 2–8 years old (past survival stage, still scrappy)

\- Location: United States (any state, with extra focus on CA, NY, TX, FL, IL)



\## Service Offerings \& Pricing

\- Basic Draft Package: $99 flat fee  

&nbsp; (Full submission-ready draft + instructions for Amber Grant or similar)

\- Premium Draft + Guidance: $199–$299  

&nbsp; (Deeper storytelling, multiple grants, strategy call)

\- Monthly Retainer: $199–$399/month  

&nbsp; (Ongoing grant monitoring + 1–2 drafts per month)

\- Success Fee (optional later): 10% of won amount



Business model: Client fills a short Google Form → we use AI + human refinement to write a strong, heartfelt application → client submits themselves on the grant portal. We take zero liability.



\## Unique Positioning ("Grant Virgin" Scoring)

We don’t just write grants — we specifically target “Grant Virgins”: eligible businesses that have never applied because they are disorganized or unaware.



Key signals we look for:

\- Outdated or free-tier website (Wix free, old WordPress, no SSL)

\- Dead or missing social media / no LinkedIn company page

\- Business email is @gmail.com / @hotmail.com

\- Google Business Profile unclaimed or almost no reviews

\- No accountant, no awards, no press mentions

\- Business name is “\[Owner’s First Name] Bakery/Cafe/Shop”



Businesses scoring 65+ on this system are our ideal clients.



\## Tone \& Voice

\- Professional but warm and encouraging

\- Helpful expert, never pushy

\- Focus on “we make it easy” and “you keep 100% of your time”

\- Always emphasize: no equity lost, no loans, no red tape, fast turnaround
