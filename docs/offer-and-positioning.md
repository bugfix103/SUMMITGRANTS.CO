## Summit Grant Solutions – Core Offer & Positioning

### 1. One-Sentence Positioning (Internal)

Summit Grant Solutions helps **women-owned (50%+) food, beverage, and retail businesses in the United States, aged 2–8 years with 1–10 employees**, win **private grants like the Amber Grant** by turning their story and goals into a **submission-ready application draft**, so they can access “free money” without drowning in paperwork.

### 2. Ideal Client Profile (ICP)

- **Ownership**: 50%+ woman-owned, for-profit business  
- **Niches**: 
  - Food & Beverage – cafes, bakeries, restaurants, catering, food trucks, coffee shops
  - Retail – boutiques, small shops, local markets, specialty stores
- **Stage**: 
  - In business **2–8 years** (past initial survival, still scrappy and under-resourced)
  - Team size **1–10 employees** (can stretch to 50 max)
- **Location**: 
  - United States, all states, with extra focus on **CA, NY, TX, FL, IL**
- **Online “Grant Virgin” Signals** (65+ score is ideal):
  - Outdated or free-tier website (Wix free, old WordPress, no SSL)
  - Weak/missing social media, no LinkedIn company page
  - Generic email like `@gmail.com`, `@hotmail.com`, `@yahoo.com`
  - Almost no Google reviews, unclaimed Google Business Profile
  - No visible awards, grants, investors, or press coverage
  - Business name is “[Owner First Name] Bakery/Cafe/Shop”

These are business owners who are **strong operators but weak on paperwork** and likely have **never applied for a private grant before**.

### 3. Core Service Packages

#### 3.1 Basic Amber Grant Draft – **$99**

- 1x submission-ready draft for **Amber Grant** (starting with Food & Beverage category in March)
- Turn client’s short intake form into a strong, heartfelt narrative using AI + human editing
- Clear instructions on:
  - How to submit on the official Amber Grant website
  - Where to paste each answer
  - How to pay the program’s application fee
- Delivery target: **within 24–48 hours** after the client completes the intake form
- No promises of winning, no legal responsibility – **we provide the draft, client submits**

#### 3.2 Premium Grant Draft + Guidance – **$199–$299**

- Everything in the Basic package, plus:
  - Deeper storytelling and positioning for the business
  - Option to adapt the core narrative to **1–2 additional private grants** (e.g., Hello Alice, IFundWomen)
  - Light strategy guidance by email/DM (which grant to prioritize, timeline)
- Ideal for clients who are serious about pursuing more than one opportunity in the next 1–3 months.

#### 3.3 Monthly Grant Retainer – **$199–$399/month**

- Ongoing **grant monitoring** for relevant private grants (Amber Grant + Hello Alice + others)
- **1–2 submission-ready drafts per month** included
- Priority turnaround and support for clients who want a steady rhythm of applications
- Optional **success fee** layer: **10% of any grant amount won**, only if the client wants deeper partnership later.

### 4. Non-Negotiable Rules (Internal)

- We **write drafts**, the **client submits** on the grant website.
- We never guarantee wins; we only promise a **strong, submission-ready draft**.
- We focus messaging on:
  - “We make it easy”
  - “You keep 100% of your time for your business”
  - “No equity, no loans, just a better shot at free money”
- We always use **Grant Virgin thinking**:
  - Prioritize businesses that clearly **have not** been applying for grants yet.
  - Avoid over-optimized, slick brands that are likely already working with agencies/consultants.

### 5. External Value Proposition (Client-Facing Bullets)

Use variations of these bullets across emails, LinkedIn, and landing pages:

- **“Grant-ready story in 48 hours”** – We turn your story and goals into a submission-ready Amber Grant draft, so you can apply without staring at a blank page.
- **“You run the bakery, we handle the paperwork”** – You answer a few simple questions, we handle the writing. No calls, no Zooms, no long forms.
- **“Private grants, not bank loans”** – No equity, no debt, no guarantees required. Just a stronger chance at $5k–$10k+ from private programs.
- **“Built for busy women owners”** – We focus on real small businesses with messy websites and busy owners, not perfect Instagram brands.
- **“Flat fee, no hidden strings”** – $99 for a full Amber Grant draft. You keep 100% of the money if you win.

### 6. How To Use This Document

- When drafting **outreach copy**, always:
  - Mention **women-owned**, **food/retail**, and **private grants like Amber Grant**.
  - Make it clear we **draft**, they **submit**.
  - Highlight **speed**, **simplicity**, and **no equity/loans**.
- When qualifying leads, use the **ICP + Grant Virgin signals** to decide if someone is a **priority prospect**.
